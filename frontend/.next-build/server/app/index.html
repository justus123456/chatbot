Yes. The assistant is designed for students across departments and levels.

You can still use them, but the important information can be organised here so you do not miss deadlines in noisy group chats.

Can it help with lecture notes?

Yes. The study tools are planned for summaries, flashcards, and practice questions from uploaded notes.

The question can be sent to an admin or school staff member instead of the system guessing.
